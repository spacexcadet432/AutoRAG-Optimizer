Company Overview

TechNova Solutions is a mid-sized technology company founded in 2012. The company specializes in cloud infrastructure, AI solutions, and enterprise automation tools.

Refund Policy

Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, refunds are not permitted. Refund requests must be submitted through the customer portal along with proof of purchase.

Support Information

Technical support is available via email at support@technova.com. Premium customers can also access 24/7 live chat support. Standard response time for email inquiries is 24–48 hours.

Leadership Team

The CEO of TechNova Solutions is Elena Martinez. The CTO is Daniel Cho, who leads the AI research division. The CFO is Priya Raman, overseeing financial operations since 2018.

Product Details

TechNova offers three main products:
1. NovaCloud – A scalable cloud hosting solution.
2. NovaAI – An AI analytics platform for enterprise clients.
3. NovaFlow – A workflow automation tool designed for mid-sized businesses.

Subscription Plans

There are three subscription tiers:
- Basic Plan: Includes NovaCloud only.
- Pro Plan: Includes NovaCloud and NovaAI.
- Enterprise Plan: Includes all three products along with dedicated support.

Security and Compliance

TechNova complies with GDPR and SOC 2 standards. All customer data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Two-factor authentication is mandatory for Enterprise Plan users.

Office Locations

The company headquarters is located in Berlin, Germany. There are regional offices in Toronto, Canada and Singapore.
